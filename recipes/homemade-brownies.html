Homemade Brownies

A simple and delicious dessert. easy to make and loaded with chocolatey goodness.

Brownies are the dessert for a lazy chocolate lover. they are easy and fast to make while being loaded with amazing chocolate.

this is a recipe for glossy, fudgy and filled with chocolate flavor. they are so fast and easy to make you will have no issues even baking them on a weeknight.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder, sifted if lumpy
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar, sifted if lumpy
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 3/4 teaspoons sea salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup canola oil or extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325Â°F. Lightly spray an 8x8 baking dish with cooking spray and line it with parchment paper. Spray the parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, wisk together the eggs, olive oil, water and vanilla.
  4. Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the preoared pan and use a spatula to smooth the top. bake for 40 to 48 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out with only a few crumbs attached.Cool completely before slicing. Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days. These also freeze well.
